## Navigation

Basic mouse and keyboard actions to navigate your canvas.

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dvu6ueq2b/image/upload/v1755727420/Docs%20images/canvas_vgm6f9.png)

- Left click: select, interact
- Right click: currently isn't working
- left click + drag: pan the canvas
- Middle mouse scroll wheel: Zoom in and out
