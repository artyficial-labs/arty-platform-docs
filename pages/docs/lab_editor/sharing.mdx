## Collaboration and Sharing

There are 3 ways to share your lab. Please do not share your lab with anyone you do not want in your lab. 

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dvu6ueq2b/image/upload/v1755727608/Docs%20images/share_l3xyl5.png)

- Copy and send a link
- Share to library
- Invite/Manage Collaborators

## Invite / Manage collaborators

![Image](https://res.cloudinary.com/dvu6ueq2b/image/upload/v1755727573/Docs%20images/colab-share_mkz8ai.png)

To invite people to collaborate on your lab they wil have to already have an account. You can also give your collaborators roles on your lab. All this can be managed at anytime if you need to make updates or remove collaborators.
