## Video Workflow

To create a video generation workflow you will need 2 nodes

- Video Gen Model Node
- Video Preview Node 

![Image](/video-workflow.png)

### Video Generation Node 

Creates videos from text prompts. Users can specify duration (up to 6 seconds) and FPS (frames per second) settings. The generated videos display within the node for preview.

### Video Preview Node 

Displays and plays videos generated by the Video Node or other sources. Includes download functionality and properly handles various video formats and aspect ratios.
