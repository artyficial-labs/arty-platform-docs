# Welcome to Artyficial Research. 
 
This will help you get started on using our platform for research or projects. We are working on closed and open models like llama and chatgpt.
Please note that Artyficial Research is a guide on how to interact, learn and/or use generative ai models on Artyficial Labs. It has not been tested on other platforms.

## What is Artyficial Labs?

A **simple**, **powerful** and **flexible** Creative AI research platform to empower creatives with generative ai tools and assistants. 
The platform is an on-going research implementation of how multi-modals are impacting the creative and creator sectors of product and brand development.
